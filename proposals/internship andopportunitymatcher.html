internship Matchers

Proposed by: Racha, Zubeda, Ilgin.

Description

A web application designed to connect students with internship oppurtunities. Students can create profiles listing their skills, experience, interests, while companies can post available interships with required skills. System that suggest potential matches, helping students find internships that suit their abilities and companies find suitable candidates.

Resources

  • Student list
  • Company list
  • Match list
    • Admin dashboard
    • Create/edit/delete student
    • accept/delete match

Users

  • Students
  • Companies

ForecastFit

Proposed by: Aayush

Description

This weather app does exactly what it says on its title. Shows the users the weather and gives out a outfit recommendation going off of the weather. Users can browse through days of the current week and check out the weather. They will be able to plan and get an idea of what their outfit should be depending on what it recommends. It will need an administrator who checks the website api daily and updates it daily. They will also approve the stylists decision. Guest users who are first time users and can view todays weather and get basic outfit recommendations. What they won't be able to do is save outfits. Regular users which will be most users, they can save their outfits and create a profile. They will also be able to plan their outfits as they will be able to check the current weeks instead of just 1 day. Stylist or fashion creators will also be needed to give out outfit recommendations based on the weather.

Resources

  • Weather data APIs
  • Clothing data
  • Location services
